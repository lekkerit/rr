# Bots in Public — Course Overview

**Format:** 10-day email course on Substack
**Week 1:** Free (Days 1–5) — The Diagnosis
**Week 2:** Paid €99 (Days 6–10) — The Cure
**Hook:** ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude — they all have amnesia. Week 1 is the diagnosis. Week 2 is the cure.
**Outcome:** A working, stateful Claude Code project on GitHub Pages
**Incentive:** 1 month Claude Pro gifted on paid signup (~€20)
**Net per student:** €79

## Gift Workflow (per new paid subscriber)

Do this within 24 hours of each new paid subscription:

1. Go to claude.ai/gift
2. Select Claude Pro, 1 month
3. Enter the subscriber's email (visible in Substack dashboard)
4. Complete purchase (~€20)
5. Send welcome message in private community confirming gift is on the way

> At 30+ students: automate via Zapier — Substack new paid subscriber → email reminder with their address.
