Yesterday you built your CLAUDE.md. Today you use it for the first time in a real Claude conversation — and feel the difference immediately.

**Step 1 — Open a new Claude conversation**

Go to claude.ai. Click New conversation. Don't type anything yet.

**Step 2 — Paste your CLAUDE.md first**

Open your `bots-in-public` folder. Open `CLAUDE.md`. Copy everything. Paste it at the top of the Claude text box.

**Step 3 — Now type your prompt**

> Read my project brief above. Then help me write a 3-sentence summary of what I'm building and who it's for.

Notice the difference. Claude knows who you are. You didn't have to explain anything. That's stateful AI.

Tomorrow: we look at a tool called Cowork — a stepping stone between the browser and Claude Code. It explains something important about where the memory really lives.
