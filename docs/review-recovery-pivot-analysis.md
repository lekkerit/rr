**Date:** February 4, 2026

## Executive Summary

**Key Finding:** Only ~10% of independent restaurants in Hilversum have ≤3 star ratings (excluding chains). Targeting struggling restaurants presents significant challenges around client quality, pricing power, and market size.

**Recommendation:** **PIVOT** from "fixing struggling restaurants" to "helping successful restaurants grow through professional review management."

**Impact of Pivot:**
- Market size: +312% (17 → 70 restaurants in Hilversum)
- Pricing power: +75% (€249 → €349 average)
- Client quality: Vastly improved (growing vs struggling)
- 6-month MRR potential: +439% (€1,296 → €6,980)

## Appendix: Supporting Data

### Evidence for Review Response Impact

**Harvard Business School Study (2016):**
- Responding to customer reviews leads to better ratings
- Effect is stronger for independent restaurants vs chains
- Benefit increases with lower initial ratings (up to 3.5 stars)

**BrightLocal Consumer Review Survey (2024):**
- 87% of consumers read online reviews for local businesses
- 79% trust online reviews as much as personal recommendations
- 94% say positive reviews make them more likely to use a business

**Google My Business Statistics:**
- Businesses with complete profiles get 7x more clicks than incomplete profiles
- 64% of consumers use Google Maps to find local businesses
- Businesses that respond to reviews get 35% more clicks

### Market Research Tools Used

**Apify Google Maps Scraper:**
- Cost: ~€4 per 1,000 results
- Data extracted: Name, rating, review count, address, phone, hours
- Hilversum search yielded: ~250 total restaurants
- Filter applied: Removed chains (KFC, Papa John's, etc.)
- Result: ~175 independent restaurants
