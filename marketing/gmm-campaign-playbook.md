# Google Maps Messaging Campaign Playbook

**Goal:** 100 restaurants contacted → 10 customers signed
**Timeline:** 2-3 weeks
**Cost:** €0

---

## Campaign Overview

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Messages sent | 100 |
| Response rate | 35-40% |
| Interested | 15-20 |
| Demos booked | 10-12 |
| Customers signed | 10 |

---

## Daily Workflow (1 hour/day)

| Time | Task |
|------|------|
| 9:00-9:30 | Find 20 restaurants (4.0-4.5★, Het Gooi) |
| 9:30-9:45 | Personalize messages |
| 9:45-10:00 | Send via Google Maps |
| Throughout day | Respond to replies (15-30 min) |

---

## Prospect Criteria

| Filter | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
| Rating | 4.0-4.5★ |
| Location | Het Gooi (Hilversum, Bussum, Naarden, Laren, Huizen) |
| Reviews | 15+ total |
| Type | Modern/international (English-friendly) |

---

## Message Template

```
Hoi [Name],

Ik zag dat [Restaurant] 4.2★ heeft - mooi resultaat!

Ik help restaurants in Het Gooi meer reserveringen uit reviews halen.
Harvard bewijs: professionele responses = +35% meer conversies.

Mag ik 2 voorbeeldresponses voor [Restaurant] sturen?
(Gratis, geen verplichtingen)

Interesse?
```

---

## Response Handling

| Their Reply | Your Action |
|-------------|-------------|
| "Ja" / interested | Send 2 custom response examples within 2 hours |
| "Hoeveel kost dit?" | "Eerst gratis voorbeelden, dan €349/maand" → Send examples |
| "Hoe werkt het?" | Brief explanation + offer examples |
| No reply (48 hrs) | Follow-up: "Mag ik 1 voorbeeldreactie sturen?" |
| "Niet geïnteresseerd" | "Geen probleem, succes!" → Remove from list |

---

## Conversion Flow

```
1. GMM Response Received
   ↓
2. Send 2 Professional Response Examples (their actual reviews)
   ↓
3. If Interested → Ask: "Is onboarding in Engels OK?"
   ↓
4. If Yes → Send Typeform Questionnaire
   ↓
5. Typeform Completed → Send Payment Link (€349)
   ↓
6. Payment Received → Schedule 15-min Onboarding Call
   ↓
7. Onboarding Complete → Service Live
```

---

## Onboarding Checklist

| Step | Owner | Time |
|------|-------|------|
| Typeform questionnaire | Customer | 5 min |
| Payment (Stripe/Mollie) | Customer | 2 min |
| Google Business Profile access | Customer | 10 min |
| Brand voice setup | You | 15 min |
| First responses posted | You | 24 hrs |

**Total onboarding time:** ~1 day from payment to live

## Qualification Questions (During Chat)

| Question | Purpose | Red Flag |
|----------|---------|----------|

| "Hoeveel reviews krijg je per maand?" | Volume check | <2/month |
| "Reageer je nu op reviews?" | Current behavior | "Ja, altijd binnen 1 dag" |

## Key Messaging Rules

**DO say:**
- "Professional responses"
- "More reservations"
- "+35% conversion"
- "€349 = €900 extra revenue"

**DON'T say:**
- "AI-powered"
- "Automated"
- "Fix your reputation"
- "Damage control"
